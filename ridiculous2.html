The Most Ridiculous Rule

Madeline Voss

In elementary school, we were taking a field trip to , Philadelphia, and our bus driver did not want us to talk or sing on the bus.
Because phones were not supposed to be on the trip, we were all just sitting there in silence waiting to get off the bus.
It felt like the longest bus ride ever, and when we got off the bus we were all talking about how jealous we were of the other bus.
On the ride home, our teacher had a discussion with the bus driver, and we were allowed to have discussions.
